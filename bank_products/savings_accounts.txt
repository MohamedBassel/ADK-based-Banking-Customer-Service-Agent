Savings and Investment Products

High-Yield Savings Account
- Interest rate: 4.50% APY (Annual Percentage Yield)
- Minimum opening deposit: $100
- Monthly maintenance fee: $0
- Minimum balance requirement: None
- FDIC insured up to $250,000
- Unlimited deposits
- Up to 6 withdrawals per month (federal regulation)
- Mobile and online banking included
- No ATM card (transfers only)

Premium Money Market Account
- Interest rate: 4.75% APY on balances over $25,000
- Tiered rates: 3.50% for $10k-$25k, 4.00% for $25k-$50k, 4.75% for $50k+
- Minimum opening deposit: $10,000
- Monthly maintenance fee: $15 (waived with $25,000 minimum balance)
- Check writing privileges (up to 3 checks per month)
- ATM card included with unlimited withdrawals
- FDIC insured up to $250,000
- Mobile and online banking included

Certificates of Deposit (CDs)
- Interest rates: 
  - 3-month CD: 3.50% APY
  - 6-month CD: 4.00% APY
  - 12-month CD: 4.50% APY
  - 24-month CD: 4.25% APY
  - 60-month CD: 4.00% APY
- Minimum deposit: $1,000
- Early withdrawal penalty: 3 months interest for terms under 1 year, 6 months interest for longer terms
- FDIC insured up to $250,000
- Automatic renewal option available
- Ladder strategy recommended for flexibility

Youth Savings Account
- Interest rate: 2.00% APY
- Minimum opening deposit: $10
- Monthly maintenance fee: $0
- No minimum balance requirement
- FDIC insured up to $250,000
- Parent/guardian required for minors under 18
- Financial literacy resources included
- Transition to adult account at age 18
- Mobile app with parental controls
