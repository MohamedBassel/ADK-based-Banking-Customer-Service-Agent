Loan Products

Personal Loan
- Loan amount: $1,000 - $50,000
- Interest rate: 6.99% - 24.99% APR based on credit score
- Term length: 12 to 60 months
- Monthly payment example: $10,000 loan at 10% APR for 36 months = $323/month
- Origination fee: 1% - 5% of loan amount
- No prepayment penalty
- Requirements: Minimum credit score 620, steady income
- Typical uses: Debt consolidation, home improvements, major purchases

Home Mortgage
- Loan amount: $50,000 - $2,000,000
- Interest rate: 6.25% - 7.50% APR for 30-year fixed
- Term length: 15-year or 30-year fixed, or adjustable rate options
- Down payment: Minimum 3% for qualified buyers, 20% to avoid PMI
- Closing costs: 2% - 5% of loan amount
- Requirements: Credit score 620+, debt-to-income ratio below 43%
- Pre-approval available online in 24 hours
- First-time homebuyer programs available

Auto Loan
- Loan amount: $5,000 - $100,000
- Interest rate: 4.99% - 15.99% APR based on credit score and vehicle
- Term length: 24 to 84 months
- Down payment: Recommended 10% - 20%
- Requirements: Valid driver's license, proof of insurance, steady income
- New and used vehicles eligible
- Pre-approval available instantly online
- Special rates for electric and hybrid vehicles

Student Loan Refinancing
- Loan amount: $10,000 - $500,000
- Interest rate: 4.25% - 9.99% APR fixed or variable
- Term length: 5, 7, 10, 15, or 20 years
- No origination fees or prepayment penalties
- Requirements: Bachelor's degree, minimum credit score 650, steady income
- Can refinance federal and private student loans
- Co-signer release available after 24 on-time payments
